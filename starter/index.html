ABOUT ME

I first started getting to know a bit more about graphic design in September 2021, when I set up a little takeaway business and designed my own logo, menu and flyers. (HUS Burgers) I started my studies on Udemy and took a course on Adobe Illustrator. Shortly after I finished my first course, I subscribed to Skillshare and carried on learning new skills starting with Adobe Photoshop then onto InDesign, Premiere Pro, XD and After Effects. In one year, I made a lot of progress with these programs and learnt how to use most of them confidently. Now I am learning web development with EdX, and I am on my way to my new career.
